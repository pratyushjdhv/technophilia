"Technophilia" refers to a strong enthusiasm or love for technology and technological advancements. It describes an individual's keen interest, passion, or affinity for all things related to technology, gadgets, digital innovations, and the rapid evolution of technological tools and systems. Someone who is technophilic is typically excited about exploring, adopting, and staying abreast of the latest developments in the world of technology.
